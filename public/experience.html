as a ceremony.
Not a database flag.

They searched your profile. The search query is visible to you. You see what they were looking for before deciding anything.

Step 02

You raise your hand

If interested, you raise your hand. Before doing so, you can request confirmation of the industry — to ensure it's not your current employer.

Step 03

Both parties consent

The Peer also agrees to reveal. Until both sides agree independently — nobody's name crosses. Not a setting. The system will not permit it.

Simultaneous introduction

Both receive the same message at the same moment. Names, contact, and the query that started it. The platform recedes. The conversation is yours.
